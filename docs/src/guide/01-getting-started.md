# Getting Started with Crypto Trading

> **Timeline:** 2-4 weeks to complete this foundation
> **Investment:** Start with $0 (learn first, trade later)

## Introduction

Welcome to crypto trading. If you're reading this, you're either excited about the potential profits or you've already lost money and want to understand why. Both are valid starting points.

Let me be direct: **Most people who start trading crypto lose money.** Not because crypto is a scam, but because they skip the fundamentals, over-leverage, and let emotions drive decisions.

This guide will help you avoid those mistakes.

## What is Crypto Trading?

Crypto trading is the act of buying and selling cryptocurrencies to profit from price movements. Unlike traditional investing (buy and hold for years), trading involves:

- **Shorter timeframes** - Minutes to months, not years
- **Active management** - Regular monitoring and decisions
- **Technical analysis** - Charts, patterns, indicators
- **Higher risk** - More volatility = more opportunity AND danger

### Types of Crypto Trading

1. **Day Trading**
   - Buy and sell within the same day
   - Requires full-time attention
   - High stress, high time commitment
   - **Not recommended for beginners**

2. **Swing Trading**
   - Hold positions for days to weeks
   - Captures medium-term trends
   - More manageable for part-time traders
   - **Recommended starting point**

3. **Position Trading**
   - Hold for weeks to months
   - Based on longer-term trends
   - Less time intensive
   - **Also good for beginners**

4. **Scalping**
   - Dozens of trades per day
   - Profits from tiny price movements
   - Extremely demanding
   - **Avoid until you're consistently profitable**

5. **HODLing (Not Really Trading)**
   - Buy and hold for years
   - Minimal active management
   - Lower stress but requires conviction
   - **Safest for most people**

## Before You Trade: Critical Prerequisites

### 1. Financial Stability

**DO NOT start trading if:**

- ‚ùå You have high-interest debt (credit cards, payday loans)
- ‚ùå You don't have 3-6 months emergency fund
- ‚ùå You need this money for rent, food, or bills
- ‚ùå You're trading to solve financial problems

**You're ready to start if:**

- ‚úÖ All essential expenses are covered
- ‚úÖ Emergency fund is established
- ‚úÖ You have genuinely disposable income
- ‚úÖ Losing 100% won't affect your lifestyle

### 2. Time Commitment

**Minimum time requirements:**

- **Learning phase:** 2-4 hours daily for 3+ months
- **Active trading:** 1-3 hours daily ongoing
- **Position monitoring:** 30 minutes daily minimum

If you can't commit this time, consider passive investing instead.

### 3. Emotional Readiness

Can you handle:

- Losing 30% of your investment in a day?
- Missing a 5x gain you almost took?
- Being wrong publicly if you share trades?
- Months of effort with no profits?

If no, that's honest. Trading might not be for you, and that's okay.

Before trading crypto, understand what you're trading:

   - How pairs affect profits
   - Cross-pair arbitrage basics

## Step 2: Setting Up Your Trading Environment (Week 2)

### Choosing an Exchange

Your exchange is your trading headquarters. Choose wisely.

**Top Tier Exchanges (Recommended):**

1. **Coinbase / Coinbase Pro**
   - Best for: US beginners
   - Pros: Regulated, insured, user-friendly
   - Cons: Higher fees, fewer coins
   - Fees: 0.5% (basic) to 0.05% (Pro)

2. **Kraken**
   - Best for: US/EU traders seeking security
   - Pros: Never been hacked, good support
   - Cons: Interface can be confusing
   - Fees: 0.16% to 0.26%

3. **Binance / Binance.US**
   - Best for: Global traders (not US)
   - Pros: Lowest fees, most coins, liquidity
   - Cons: Regulatory issues, complex
   - Fees: 0.1% (0.075% with BNB)

4. **Bybit**
   - Best for: Derivatives trading
   - Pros: Great for futures, good bonuses
   - Cons: Not available everywhere
   - Fees: 0.1% maker, 0.06% taker

**Avoid:**

- Unknown exchanges with "amazing deals"
- Exchanges without proper licensing
- Platforms with withdrawal issues (check Reddit)
- Anywhere requiring you to send crypto first

### Exchange Checklist

Before choosing:

- ‚úÖ Check if legal in your country

### Security Setup (CRITICAL)

**1. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**

```
DO NOT use SMS 2FA (SIM swap attacks)
DO use:
- Google Authenticator
- Authy
- Hardware keys (YubiKey)
```

**2. Email Security**

- Unique email for each exchange
- Strong, unique password (use password manager)
- Enable email 2FA
- Never click links in crypto emails

**3. Withdrawal Whitelist**

- Enable withdrawal address whitelisting
- Add 24-48 hour withdrawal delays
- Use anti-phishing codes

**4. Password Management**

- Use Bitwarden, 1Password, or LastPass
- 20+ character passwords
- Never reuse passwords
- Store recovery codes offline

## Step 3: Paper Trading (Weeks 3-4 minimum)

**CRITICAL:** Do NOT skip paper trading. This is where you learn without losing real money.

### What is Paper Trading?

Simulated trading with fake money. You:

- Make all the same decisions
- Execute trades on paper
- Track results manually
- Learn from mistakes without cost

### How to Paper Trade

**Method 1: Manual Tracking (Recommended)**

```
1. Create a spreadsheet
2. Start with fake $10,000
3. "Buy" and "sell" at real prices
4. Track every trade with:
   - Entry date/time
   - Entry price
   - Position size
   - Reason for entry
   - Exit date/time
   - Exit price
   - Profit/loss
   - Emotions felt
   - Lessons learned
```

**Method 2: Exchange Demo Accounts**

- Bybit has testnet
- Some platforms offer paper trading
- Less educational than manual tracking
- Good for learning platform mechanics

### Paper Trading Goals

**Week 3-4 Objectives:**

- Execute 20+ trades
- Win or lose, learn from each
- Identify your emotional triggers
- Test different strategies
- Learn order types
- Understand fee impact

**Success Metrics:**

- NOT profitability (yet)
- Following a system consistently
- Cutting losses quickly
- Journaling every trade
- Staying disciplined

### Common Paper Trading Mistakes

‚ùå **Taking it less seriously** - "It's just fake money"

- Solution: Treat it like real money you'll lose

‚ùå **Taking huge risks** - "Let me try 50x leverage"

- Solution: Trade like you would with real money

‚ùå **Not tracking properly** - Sloppy records

- Solution: Detailed journal or no trade at all

‚ùå **Giving up after losses** - "This doesn't work"

- Solution: Losses are data, not failure

## Common Beginner Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Going All-In

**What happens:** New trader puts 100% in one coin
**Result:** When it drops 30%, panic sells for big loss
**Solution:** Never risk more than 5% on one trade

### Mistake 2: FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)

**What happens:** Coin pumps 50%, trader buys at top
**Result:** Immediate 20% loss, holds hoping for recovery
**Solution:** Wait for pullbacks, better entries always come

### Mistake 3: No Stop-Loss

**What happens:** "I'll just hold until it recovers"
**Result:** -10% becomes -50% becomes -80%
**Solution:** Always use stop-losses, protect capital

### Mistake 4: Revenge Trading

**What happens:** Loses money, immediately makes bigger trade to "win it back"
**Result:** Emotional decisions lead to bigger losses
**Solution:** Take a break after losses, follow the plan

### Mistake 5: Following Influencers Blindly

**What happens:** "Crypto guru" says buy, trader buys without research
**Result:** Guru already sold, trader holds the bag
**Solution:** Do your own research (DYOR), always

### Mistake 6: Trading Too Much

**What happens:** Makes 20 trades per day trying to catch every move
**Result:** Death by fees and bad decisions
**Solution:** Quality over quantity, wait for setups

### Mistake 7: Ignoring Fees

**What happens:** Makes many small trades, doesn't track fees
**Result:** "I'm profitable!" No, fees ate everything.
**Solution:** Calculate fees in every trade, they matter

## Red Flags and Scams to Avoid

### Common Scams

**1. Pump and Dump Groups**

```
"Join our VIP group! 1000% guaranteed!"
"We pump coins together!"
"Get in before announcement!"
```

Reality: Admins buy first, you're exit liquidity

**2. Fake Gurus**

```
"I made $1M in 6 months, here's my course for $2,997"
Rents Lamborghini for photo
No verifiable track record
```

Reality: They make money selling courses, not trading

**3. Ponzi Schemes**

```
"Invest with us, guaranteed 10% monthly returns"
"Refer friends for bonuses"
Can't explain how they generate returns
```

Reality: New investor money pays old investors (until it doesn't)

**4. Fake Exchanges**

```
Amazing deposit bonuses
Can deposit but can't withdraw
Support goes silent
Website disappears
```

Reality: They steal your funds

**5. Impersonation Scams**

```
"Vitalik Buterin" DMs you about ETH
"Elon Musk" BTC giveaway
"Exchange support" asks for seed phrase
```

Reality: No legitimate person DMs you first

### How to Spot Scams

üö© **Red Flags:**

- Guaranteed returns
- Urgency ("Act now!")
- Can't withdraw
- No regulation/licenses
- Anonymous team
- Too good to be true
- Asks for seed phrase
- Unsolicited DMs

‚úÖ **Green Flags:**

- Transparent team
- Clear business model
- Regulated where possible
- Long track record
- Active, honest community
- Admits risks openly
- No guarantees
