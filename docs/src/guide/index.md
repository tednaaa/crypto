## ğŸ’¡ Core Principles from 15+ Years

### The 80/20 Rule

- 80% of success comes from risk management and psychology
- 20% comes from technical analysis and strategy
- Most traders focus on the wrong 20%

### The Reality of Trading

- **Year 1:** You will likely lose money. Consider it tuition.
- **Year 2-3:** You might break even. This is actually great progress.
- **Year 4+:** Consistent profitability becomes possible with discipline.

### What Actually Matters

1. **Position sizing** - More important than entry price
2. **Risk management** - More important than being right
3. **Consistency** - More important than big wins
4. **Patience** - More important than activity
5. **Survival** - More important than profits

## ğŸ“ˆ Success Metrics to Track

Don't just track P&L. Track these:

1. **Win Rate** - % of profitable trades
2. **Risk/Reward Ratio** - Average win vs average loss
3. **Maximum Drawdown** - Largest peak-to-trough decline
4. **Sharpe Ratio** - Risk-adjusted returns
5. **Trade Frequency** - Are you overtrading?
6. **Emotional State** - Journal your feelings per trade
7. **Rule Adherence** - Did you follow your system?
